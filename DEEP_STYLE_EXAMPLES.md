## Tips for Best Results

### Sample Excerpt Quality:
- ✅ **Minimum**: 50 characters
- ✅ **Recommended**: 200-500 words
- ✅ **Ideal**: Multiple paragraphs showing your natural writing flow
- ✅ **Content**: Write about anything - the style matters, not the topic

### What Gets Matched:
- Sentence structure and length patterns
- Vocabulary preferences and word choice
- Grammar patterns (contractions, passive voice, etc.)
- Punctuation style and frequency
- Transition words and connectors
- Tone and formality level

### What Doesn't Get Matched:
- Specific content or facts from your sample
- Personal opinions or viewpoints
- Proper nouns or specialized terminology (unless in lexicon)
- Spelling errors or typos
